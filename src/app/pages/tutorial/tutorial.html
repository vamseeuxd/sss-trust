Welcome to SSS-Trust APP

Sri Subramanyeswara Swamy Trust is a charitable Organization based in Mangalagiri. A unique highlight of the app is the Nowuluru Naagendra Swami Vaari Putta Temple History Page, where users can explore the rich history, traditions, and significance of this revered temple. Additionally, the app includes a dedicated section for Events conducted by SSS-Trust, such as medical camps, where users can get detailed information about past and upcoming events aimed at serving the community.

What is SSS-Trust App?

The SSS-Trust Charitable Organization Mobile App is a comprehensive platform designed to connect users with the noble initiatives undertaken by SSS-Trust. The app features an About Page that provides insights into the trust's mission, vision, and the charitable work it has been involved in over the years. The Service Page showcases the various services and programs offered by the trust, focusing on community welfare, healthcare, and education.
